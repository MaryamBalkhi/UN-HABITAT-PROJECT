URBAN OCTOBER

Each year, the United Nations invites the world and the wider public to celebrate Urban October, a month of reflection, dialogue, and actions on sustainable urban development.

Urban October begins with World Habitat Day, observed this year on 6 October 2025, highlighting the state of cities and communities and the fundamental right to adequate shelter and housing.

Urban October concludes with World Cities Day on 31 October, celebrating progress and partnerships to build more inclusive, resilient, and sustainable urban futures.
